## Background

Two distinct 25-year careers:

### Career 1: Media, Publishing & Advertising (NYC)
Magazine publishing executive, including work at Rolling Stone on the famous "Perception vs. Reality" advertising campaign. Deep experience in media strategy, audience psychology, brand messaging, and advertising — spanning early internet and online media.

### Career 2: Fiduciary Financial Advisor
25+ years as a licensed fiduciary advisor specializing in late-stage retirement planning. Worked with thousands of clients in high-income markets (Westchester, Marin County). Observed firsthand how even sophisticated, successful people make poor Social Security decisions due to anxiety and misinformation.

**No longer licensed.** This is a feature, not a limitation — it opens the door to education, coaching, and software without the constraints of advisory compliance.

**Lived the strategy.** Waited until 70 to file for Social Security. Not theorizing — living the results.
