# Business & Work — Ret1re.com

## Overview

**Ret1re.com** helps people make strategic Social Security filing decisions to maximize lifetime guaranteed income. Everything lives under the Ret1re umbrella.

**Status:** Pre-launch. All projects at ~95% completion.

## The Core Insight

Social Security is often the largest single financial asset most Americans will ever control — equivalent to $600K–$1.2M+ using the 4% withdrawal rule — yet decisions are routinely made under anxiety, impulse, and misinformation.

## Target Audience

The "Strategic 15%" — educated professionals who change behavior when shown clear evidence. Not hand-holders. Evidence-movers.

## Key Reframe

The 62–70 window is not "waiting." It's **earning** — each month of delayed filing actively builds guaranteed lifetime income. The language matters enormously.

## Key Frameworks

- **Two Bridges** — contrasting strategic planning vs. early filing decisions
- **RISE and SHINE Method** — emotional journey (Reality, Impulse, Strategy, Execute) + logical implementation (Strategy, Hybrid, Income, Navigate, Execute)
- **K.I.N.D. Platform** — Knowledge, Income, Navigate, Decision
- **Retirement Languages** — how different people process retirement decisions
- **One Month at a Time** — gamified calculator showing income gained per month of delayed filing
