# Social Security News Research — 2026-03-08

## Headlines & Policy

- **2.8% COLA for 2026** — average $56/month increase, but Medicare Part B premium rose to $201.90 (up $17.90), netting ~$38 for most recipients. Married couples average $3,208/month.
- **Earnings limits for 2026** — under-FRA beneficiaries face $24,480 annual limit ($1 withheld per $2 over). Those reaching FRA in 2026: $65,160 limit ($1 per $3 over until FRA month). No limit post-FRA.
- **FRA update** — now 66 years and 10 months for those born in 1959 (effective from late 2025 announcement).
- **SS tax wage cap** — increased by $7,500 for 2026 ($184,500 taxable max).
- **Trust fund depletion** — CBO now projects 2032 (one year earlier than prior estimates). No congressional action or reform legislation in the past 30 days.
- **SSA operations** — surpassed 100 million my Social Security accounts (Feb 9). Seventh Annual Slam the Scam Day (Mar 5). Expedited retroactive payments announced for millions.
- **No major legislative changes** in the Feb-March 2026 window.

Sources: [AARP](https://www.aarp.org/social-security/biggest-2026-changes/), [SSA COLA Facts](https://www.ssa.gov/news/en/cola/factsheets/2026.html), [Kiplinger](https://www.kiplinger.com/retirement/social-security/changes-coming-to-social-security-in-2026), [SSA Press Releases](https://www.ssa.gov/news/en/press/releases/index.html)
